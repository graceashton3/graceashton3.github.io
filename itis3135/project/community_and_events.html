Upcoming Events

At Bridge to Abilities, we host a variety of events throughout the year that are designed to bring together individuals and families in the special needs community. Whether it’s a support group meeting, a recreational activity, or a community gathering, we aim to create spaces for connection and learning.

  • Family Picnic Day – A day of fun, games, and socializing for families in the special needs community. Date: June 12th, 2025.
  • Caregiver Support Group – A safe space for parents and caregivers to share experiences and gain support. Date: July 22nd, 2025.
  • Advocacy Workshop – Learn how to advocate for your child and gain access to important resources. Date: August 15th, 2025.

Community Engagement

We believe that community is essential for building an inclusive world. Our programs and events focus on engaging families, caregivers, and individuals in a meaningful way. We strive to create lasting connections and support systems that go beyond just attending events but foster a long-term network of empowerment.
