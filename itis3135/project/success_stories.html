Story 1: A New Beginning

This is the story of Sarah and her son Jason. When they first reached out to Bridge to Abilities, Sarah was feeling overwhelmed and uncertain about the future. But through our programs and community support, Sarah and Jason have learned how to navigate the world in new ways. Jason now participates in a local support group, and Sarah has found a network of other parents who truly understand her journey. Today, they are thriving together, and their story inspires countless others.

Story 2: A Path to Independence

Alex, a young man with autism, struggled to find meaningful employment opportunities. With the help of Bridge to Abilities, Alex developed important life skills and gained confidence through our employment training program. He was eventually hired at a local bookstore and is now able to live independently. His journey is a testament to the power of support, persistence, and community.
