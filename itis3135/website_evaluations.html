Website Evaluations

Apple

Apple has a very clear and user freindly website. Everything is very visible and easy to read.A great website for everyone.

  • Contrast: Apple uses a high contrast between text and background, making content easy to read.
  • Repetition: Apple maintains consistency across its pages with repeated fonts, colors, and layouts.
  • Alignment: The site follows a strict grid layout, ensuring all elements are perfectly alignment.
  • Proximity: Related elements are grouped together logically, making it easy to scan product information and navigate through the site.

Pacific Northwest X-Ray Inc.

This website serves as a catalog for X-ray equipment; however, the website is not very clear or user friendly. Everything is dark and hard to read. The designs and color choices could be way better.

  • Contrast: The text and background have insufficient contrast, making it harder to read, especially in some sections.
  • Repetition: There is no clear consistency in layout and design, making navigation feel disjointed.
  • Alignment: Elements are misaligned, which affects the overall presentation.
  • Proximity: Related elements are not grouped well, making it difficult to scan through information.
