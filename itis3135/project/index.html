Welcome to Bridge To Abilities

Our mission is to support families, caregivers, and individuals with special needs by providing helpful resources, promoting inclusive community events, and creating a strong network of support. Together, we celebrate milestones and build bridges to opportunities.

Who We Serve

  • Parents and guardians of children with special needs
  • Individuals with special needs looking for community and resources
  • Educators and therapists supporting special needs individuals
  • Support organizations and advocacy groups
